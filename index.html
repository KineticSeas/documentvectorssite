What isDocument Vectors

We are a fully managed vector index of over 80 million scientific and medical documents designed to assist Large Language Models (LLM) in fact checking output and citing sources. Once your LLM of choice has creating a document, you can fact check the document and cite sources using Document Vectors and Kinetic Seas' libraries.

Fact Checking

Avoid LLM hallucinations by fact checking data and citing sources.

Cite Sources

Once your LLM has created a document, using Document Vectors to search for and cite sources.

Workflow Management

Integrate with other Kinetic Seas libraries to build a full workflow management system that includes documents.

Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG)

Use Document Vectors as part of your AI/RAG pipeline.

Document Classification

Use Document Vectors and Kinetic Seas libraries to classify documents as part of your workflow management.

Ready to GoBoost Your LLM Results

LLMs cannot cite the sources of the parametric data it contains. Vector Databases and the vector embedding of our scientific document library assists in fact checking and citing sources.
